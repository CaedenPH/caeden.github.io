    OPERATING SYSTEM

    After playing around with lots of different linux distributions; ubunutu, debian, fedora, I found Manjaro. Manjaro is an open-source operating system with great functionality and a powerful os. I am on Xfce Desktop Environment, as I appreciate the customisability and freedom it produces. Manjaro also has an amazing forum community, meaning you can get help whenever you need it from a range of advanced profesionals who offer their time to help you. I also saw that Manjaro had a command called mhwd - which is automatic hardware detection, and as I use an nvidia gpu this was necessary. Another perk is that Manjaro runs on top of Arch, a complex but fascinating OS, and Manjaro adds some simplicity to it while still retaining the useful features. I hope to stay with Manjaro as long as possible.
